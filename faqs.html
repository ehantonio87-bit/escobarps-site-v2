Frequently Asked Questions

How much does pressure washing cost?+
Pricing varies by project size and type. A standard driveway starts around $100–$200. House washing typically runs $200–$500 depending on size. We always provide a free, no-obligation estimate before any work begins.
Is soft washing safe for my roof?+
Yes! Soft washing uses low-pressure water mixed with professional-grade cleaning solution. It's actually safer than high-pressure washing for roofs, and it kills algae at the root so it takes longer to come back — typically 2–4 years.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Absolutely. Escobar Property Services is fully licensed and insured for all services we provide in Georgia. We carry general liability insurance and workers' comp, protecting both you and our team.
How long does a typical job take?+
Most residential pressure washing jobs take 2–4 hours. House washing takes 3–6 hours. Roof washing is typically 2–3 hours. Painting projects vary by scope but we give you a clear timeline upfront.
Do I need to be home during the service?+
Not necessarily. As long as we have access to the areas being cleaned and a water source, you don't need to be present. Many customers leave us to it while they're at work.
How soon can you come out?+
We typically schedule within 1–3 business days. For urgent situations, we'll do our best to accommodate same-day or next-day service. Call us at (678) 488-2716 for fastest response.
What areas do you serve?+
We serve all of metro Atlanta including Lawrenceville, Marietta, Decatur, Smyrna, Kennesaw, Alpharetta, Roswell, Sandy Springs, Johns Creek, Duluth, and surrounding areas.
Do you offer any guarantees?+
Yes — 100% satisfaction guaranteed. If you're not happy with the results, we'll come back and make it right at no additional charge. Our reputation depends on your satisfaction.
